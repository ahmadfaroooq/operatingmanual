Their opinion affects your energy and decisions. You keep a job you hate partly to avoid tantrums. You are spending your most precious resource — time in your mid-20s with no rent, no dependents, remote work — optimising for keeping the peace. The fastest fix is not a conversation. Outreach System — Non-Negotiable

The New Standard

10 outreach messages per day. 5 days per week. 200 messages per month. At 5% close rate: 10 conversations. At 20% conversion: 2 new clients per month. Seven messages in 3 months is not a sales problem. It is an avoidance problem.

The Warming System — Before Every DM

Spend 3 days commenting meaningfully on a prospect's last 5 posts before sending a message. Warm outreach gets 3 to 5x higher reply rates. Your 7 messages got 0 replies because they were fully cold.
